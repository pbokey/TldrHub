About Us

At TldrHub, we publish high-quality articles with a brand new approach to news and analysis. Unlike other news sites, each article has a 100-word summary, called a “tldr.” Another unique feature about TldrHub is that we encourage and publish opinion pieces from various perspectives, whether that be liberal, conservative, or anything in between. TldrHub started from a goal to create a true news site that allows readers to come to their own opinions, but it soon transformed into a platform where brevity and efficiency is the name of the game.
