Millennials tend to be more concerned about the environment than older generations. This may be for several reasons — we’ve had more exposure to environmental programs than our parents’ generation, and they’re more concerned with national security and economic issues. Public opinion won’t change overnight, but there are many things we can do to push our society toward more environmentally-friendly policies: vote, be seen, be heard, and make small differences.
