Breaking: Scientists upgrade Trump from Asshole to Superasshole

May 22, 2016 - by Nathan Morse

Washington, DC — The American Political Science Association (APSA) commenced its annual convention yesterday, and experts voted earlier this morning to upgrade Donald J. Trump from “Asshole” status to “Superasshole” status. The APSA has scales on which it ranks each major political figure based on political ideology, success, and character, as determined by a majority vote of the members present at the convention. The “character” scale ranges from “Sweet Old Man” to “Superasshole.” Trump is now the first political figure in APSA history to receive a status of “Superasshole.” Senator Bernie Sanders is the currently the only politician with “Sweet Old Man” status.
