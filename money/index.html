Economics: what is that?

  • September 28, 2016
  • •
  • by Gabriel Woodruff
  • •
  • TLDR

Economics is all about decision making and looking at the big picture of what affects our decisions. It’s broken into two categories: Macroeconomics and Microeconomics. Macroeconomics looks at the big picture and explains how global events such as Brexit or presidential elections affect the economy. Microeconomics looks at details and explains everything from restaurant lines to Uber prices. A major concept of economics is supply and demand, which deals with how much of a good or service there is and how much people want it. Overall, economics helps us understand how the world works.
