Symptoms of Catching a (Travel) Bug

by Dylan Glicksman • September 11, 2016

too long; didn't read: When you catch the “travel bug,” you fall in love with traveling and everything that traveling brings. Going on trips around the world makes people more appreciative and caring, and it even forces people to be more active and to perform physical activities they wouldn’t otherwise do. Traveling also almost always brings up many unexpected problems, which can improve a person’s problem-solving skills and flexibility in other areas of life. On top of being fun and relaxing, travel makes people healthier, happier, and smarter.

WAR ON WOMBS: China's One-Child Policy

  • •

  • TLDR

While most of the Western world is fixated on increasing the availability of abortions, China is experiencing a whole other problem. Its one-child policy was a response to China’s population growth problem, but it quickly created a whole range of serious problems, including a violent culture against having more than one child, ghost children, skyrocketed costs of raising a child, a massive elderly population, and even illegal immigration. China recently changed its one-child policy to a two-child policy, but the damage has already been done, and it wasn’t worth it.
