Naps Should be Allowed in the Workplace

too long; didn't read Mid-afternoon drowsiness is part of our natural biological processes, and scientific studies have shown that even a short nap greatly boosts productivity. One study showed that a 26-minute nap improves performance by 34 percent. Although people may face some difficulties when napping at work, those can be easily overcome. The main struggle workers face today with napping at work is that their bosses prohibit it. Just as employers allow lunch and restroom breaks, they need to allow each worker to take a half-hour-maximum nap every day.
