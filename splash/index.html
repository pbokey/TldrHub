Good writing can impact the world.
TldrHub is a news platform where millennials can help each other understand the world. Every day we hear about events and problems, but no one really knows what to do. At TldrHub, we publish articles from many perspectives, not just one side. We are tired of other outlets shoving their agendas down our throats and not letting us decide our own positions for ourselves. TldrHub is a place where the new generation of writers and thinkers can come together and debate and share articles and their views on current events and other investigative reports. Feel Free, Feel Safe.
