Good writing can impact the world.
TldrHub is a news platform where millennials can help each other understand the world. Every day we hear about events and problems, but no one really knows what to do. At TldrHub, we publish articles from many perspectives, not just one side. We are tired of other outlets shoving their agendas down our throats and not letting us decide our own positions for ourselves. TldrHub is a place where the new generation of writers and thinkers can come together and debate and share articles and their views on current events and other investigative reports. July 23rd - Pranav Bokey

I came to a realization that pushing the launch date back a few days was in the best interest of not only the board members, but also to all of the writers. Our editors needed more time to edit the articles and make them the best that they can be, our writers needed more time to write their wonderful first pieces. All in all we knew that a succesful, clean, and measured launch would always trump (pun intended) a rushed and hectic launch. We hope to see you back on August 5th at 7 pm where TldrHub will finally be released to the masses!
